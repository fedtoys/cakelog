The 10 Cakemandments

I am the Lord thy Cake Log.

  1. Thou shalt have no other logs before me.
  2. Thou shalt not make unto thee any graven image or any graven cake or any image of graven cake.
  3. Thou shalt not take the name of the Lord thy Cake Log in vain.
  4. Remember the Friday lunchtimes, to keep them holy.
  5. Honour thy cakes and thy logs.
  6. Thou shalt not murder cake.
  7. Thou shalt not commit adultery with another person's cake.
  8. Thou shalt not steal cake.
  9. Thou shalt not bear false witness in the cake log.
  10. Thou shalt not covet thy neighbour's cakes or thy neighbour's logs.
